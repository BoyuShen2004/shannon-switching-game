Shannon Switching Game

Connect the red terminals or block the connection!

Current Player: Short (Connector)
Game in progress

How to Play:

  • Short (Connector): Try to create a path between the two red terminal nodes by clicking on edges to "connect" them (turn green).
  • Cut (Blocker): Try to prevent the Short player from connecting the terminals by clicking on edges to "cut" them (turn red and dashed).
  • Players alternate turns. Short goes first.
  • Short wins if they create an unbroken path of connected edges between the terminals.
  • Cut wins if they make it impossible for Short to connect the terminals.
  • Once an edge is clicked, it cannot be changed!
